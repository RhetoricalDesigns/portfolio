02The Virtual Employee

What it is

A virtual employee is a defined, always-on role I design and run for you — trained on your brand, your tools and your rules. Not a generic bot; your process, automated.

It handles the repeatable, time-eating work so your people don't have to.

What it can run

  • Inbox & inquiry triage and replies
  • Lead capture → CRM → follow-up
  • Content drafting & scheduling
  • Reporting assembly & dispatch
  • QA on site copy, links, SEO
  • Research & briefs on demand
