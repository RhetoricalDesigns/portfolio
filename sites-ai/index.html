iWhat you get
a.

AI Assistants

A chatbot that knows your business — answers FAQs, books, qualifies leads. Trained on your stuff, not the open internet's guesses.

b.

Workflow Agents

Bots that act: pull data, draft replies, update records, flag exceptions. The repetitive 3-hour task becomes a 3-second one.

c.

Virtual Employee

A named, always-on role — inbox triage, reporting, follow-ups — that reports what it did each morning. Cheaper than a hire, faster than a hire.

d.

Integrations

Connect your CRM, email, calendar, docs. The agent lives where the work is, not in a separate app nobody opens.

e.

Monitoring

Every action logged, every odd result flagged. You can see the robot's homework whenever you like.

f.

Training & Tuning

I tune prompts and guardrails as you learn what you want. The employee gets smarter, not stubborner.
